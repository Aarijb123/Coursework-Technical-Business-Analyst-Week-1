## Jobs-To-Be-Done:

- When a new case enters the system, I want to quickly understand its complexity and urgency, so I can assign the right level of expertise without delay.

- When I receive a case, I want clear background and context, so I can assess it accurately and support the customer.

- When I act on a case, I want to know what’s already been done and its current status, so I can avoid duplication and move straight to resolution.

- When I check my finances or prepare a payment, I want easy access to my balance and history, so I can manage things without delay.

- When I’m asked about automation, I want to understand its impact on workload, so I can assess its value with confidence.

- When I present a value case to leadership, I want well-founded, defensible assumptions, so I can handle scrutiny and maintain credibility.

## Top 3 JTBD Justification

1. When I act on a case, I want to know what’s already been done and its current status, so I can avoid duplication and move straight to resolution. 
- This is a core operation failure point, agents are spending time inefficiently as they are spending time duplicating work and missing follow ups leading to a reduced recovery rate. This has directly impacted the revenues as it has caused an estimated 15% revenue loss.
- The evidence to support this is that the quote "Agents spend too much time proving whether work already happened before they can act." is the most observed by different stakeholders.

2. When I receive a case, I want clear background and context, so I can assess it accurately and support the customer.
- Without providing adequate context o a case, work will be rejected or repeated leading to cases being unsolved or work having to be repeated by an agent. Also means when the portal is created if context is not provided for each case agents will not adopt it as its creating more friction instead of removing it.
- The evidence to support this is many high confidence stakeholders quote that “If unsupported cases come back with no context, agents will reject the portal”. This directly flags the risk of the portal.

3.  When I check my finances or prepare a payment, I want easy access to my balance and history, so I can manage things without delay.
- This is the clearest automation opportunity, this will remove simple queries from agents and increase efficiency as agents can be working on the complex tasks that need an agent.
- The evidence to support this is a concern a shareholder had was "Customers should be able to understand what they owe without calling first.". Also with their being over 100,000 delinquent accounts, agents are being overloaded with basic tasks.
