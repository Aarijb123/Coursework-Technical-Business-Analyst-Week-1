# Templates folder

This folder holds reusable working files for Week 1.

## Files and folders

- `traceability-matrix-template.csv` - starter traceability table linking concerns, evidence, and outputs
- `miro-board-structure.md` - recommended board frames for discovery work
- `roi-model/` - starter CSV tabs for your Excel workbook

## How to use these templates

Copy them before editing if you want to preserve a blank version.

## Tip

Do not treat the templates as the answer. They exist to reduce blank-page anxiety and keep your work structured.
