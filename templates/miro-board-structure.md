# Miro Board Structure

Use these frames to keep your Week 1 board clear and reviewable.

## Recommended frame order

1. Case summary
2. Stakeholder themes
3. Evidence table snapshot
4. JTBD canvas
5. As-Is process map
6. Pain-point overlay
7. Automation opportunity shortlist
8. To-Be sketch
9. Prioritisation matrix
10. Open questions and assumptions

## Suggested colour key

- Yellow: pain points
- Blue: needs or JTBD insights
- Pink: risks or change concerns
- Green: opportunity ideas
- Grey: evidence or source references

## Review questions for your board

- Can a reviewer follow your logic without you speaking?
- Are pain points placed near the relevant workflow step?
- Is each priority opportunity tied to evidence?
- Are open assumptions visible rather than hidden?
